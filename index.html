      KPI Formula Library

      SHRM-style HR metrics and formulas
      Core HR KPIs

      Turnover Rate

      (# of separations ÷ avg headcount) × 100

      Use HR CSV headcount and separations to track voluntary/involuntary turnover.

      Cost per Hire

      (Total recruiting cost ÷ # of hires)

      Integrate with recruiting spend to monitor acquisition efficiency.

      Absence Rate

      (# of absence days ÷ total workdays) × 100

      Combine time & attendance data with HR360 headcount.

      Training ROI

      ((Program benefit − cost) ÷ cost) × 100

      Link Knowledge Lab productivity gains to learning investments.
